### `PreItemCleanupWarning`

**Purpose**
`Sent one minute before world items are removed.`

**Parameters**

* None

**Realm**
`Server`

**Returns**
`void` — `None`

**Example**
```lua

    print("Item cleanup in 60 seconds")
end)
```
